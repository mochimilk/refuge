⊹ About

Lull into the beating heart of our parallel universe, where the bass echoes through concrete walls, and dimly lit paths leads to an immersive experience like no other. We are Refuge, tucked away in the labyrinthine depths of virtual reality, where even the misfits and outcasts can find safe haven in the welcoming arms of their new community.

⊹ Mission

Our mission is to create a safe space for diverse forms of creative expression across music, art, media, gaming, dancing, and communication. We embrace diversity and celebrate both the familiar and unfamiliar, the light and dark, the joyous and sorrowful.

We hope our space can alleviate anxiety and provide a sense of belonging. We are committed to our community and will devote our time and resources to ensure that everyone feels welcomed and valued.
